The Critics of Enlightenment Rationalism

Editors: Gene Callahan and Kenneth McIntyre

The aim of this project is to provide an overview of the many critics of Enlightenment rationalism. The essays on each thinker are intended not merely to offer an overview of that thinker, but also to place him or her in the context of this larger stream of anti-rationalist thought. Below is a tentative list of chapters, along with a proposed (in violet) or confirmed (in blue) author for the chapter.
